# Child Marriage Risk Prediction Dashboard

## Overview

This tool predicts child marriage rates at the community level in Kenya. It helps NGOs and policymakers identify high-risk areas and allocate resources effectively.

## How It Works

1. Enter community characteristics (education rates, rural population, poverty levels, etc.)
2. The model predicts the child marriage rate
3. You receive a risk tier and specific intervention recommendations
